What are the new iPhone 16 specs and features? The iPhone 16e is built for Apple Intelligence and powered by the A18 chip.1 You can shoot super-high-resolution photos with the 48MP Fusion camera. And with supersized battery life, you have more time to text, browse, and more. The iPhone 16 features the A18 Bionic chip for powerful processing, along with Camera Control, which helps you quickly access camera tools, like zoom or depth of field, so you can take the perfect shot in record time. The 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR2 display gives you a crystal clear viewing experience when you’re using FaceTime or streaming video, while the improved Ultra Wide camera with autofocus lets you takes incredibly detailed macro photos and videos. Use the 48MP Fusion camera for stunning high-resolution images and zoom in with the 2x optical-quality Telephoto. And with the power of the A18 chip, you get a big boost in battery life with up to 22 hours video playback3—all in a sturdy, aerospace-grade aluminum design that comes in five vibrant colors. The iPhone 16 Plus also has the A18 Bionic chip, with the same sturdy, aerospace-grade aluminum design in five eye-catching hues. It also features an improved Ultra Wide camera, a 48MP Fusion camera, and 2x optical quality Telephoto, along with Camera Control so you can quickly access everything you need to take exceptional pictures. And with up to 27 hours of video playback, iPhone 16 Plus has you covered with a long battery life. Featuring stunning titanium design and the groundbreaking A18 Pro chip that takes processing to the next level, iPhone 16 Pro offers a larger 6.3” Super Retina XDR display and 4K 120 fps Dolby Vision for exceptional video quality. Meanwhile, the latest-generation Photographic Styles give you greater creative flexibility, so you can make every photo even more you. It also comes in four titanium colors. The iPhone 16 Pro Max also features the A18 Pro chip with 4K 120 fps Dolby Vision, four titanium colors, the latest generation of Photographic Styles, and a super-charged CPU and GPU with bigger memory bandwidth. At 6.9”, the larger screen size makes the most of the Super Retina XDR display for all your viewing needs, but it’s still easy to stash when you’re on the go. With the most robust battery life of all the new iPhone options, you’ll get up to 33 hours of video playback.
