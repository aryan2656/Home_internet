Have Device Protection and need to make a service request?

It takes just a few minutes to send a service request, and we can get most repairs done the same day! Here’s how it works:

  1. Visit http://www.rogers.com/servicerequest, go to my Plan, log in using your MyRogers info, then go to the Device Protection tab.
  2. Tell us what happened to your device.
  3. Select from the service request options presented to you.
  4. Pay any applicable processing fees.
  5. Get your device repaired or replaced.
  6. Receive your repaired or replacement device.
