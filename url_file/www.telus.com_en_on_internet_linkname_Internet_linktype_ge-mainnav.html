Frequently Asked Questions

Our high-speed internet plans vary by location. In many areas, we now offer speeds up to 1500 Mbps (1.5 Gigabit Internet).
All plans include unlimited data and a price guarantee when you sign up for a 2-year term.
What is considered a “good”  high-speed internet connection depends on your online lifestyle, the number of people in your household and the number of connected devices in your home. 
You can never have too much speed, and it’s good to have some extra speed for when you need it. PureFibre Internet plans with speeds of 1.5 Gbps download and 940 Mbps upload will allow you to get more done quickly, ensuring you never miss out on online experiences.
Download speed means how quickly you can receive data from the internet. When you’re browsing websites or streaming music or video, it’s download speed that matters.
Upload speed means how quickly you can send data to the internet. This is important for high-quality video conferencing (including Skype and FaceTime) uploading large files and especially intensive online gaming.
Most households need approximately 700 GB internet data per month for web browsing, streaming, gaming and smart home setup. Our plan includes unlimited home internet data so you never need to worry about going over.
