    Description

    With the My TELUS app, view and pay your bills easily and securely, enable data limit notifications and manage your services anytime, anywhere - so that you can get back to what’s important to you.

    Even better, enjoy the convenience of securely logging in using facial or fingerprint recognition. Have a question? Feel supported with TELUS Assist chatbot 24/7 to answer your questions, even while you’re on the go.

    Easily access your account to:
    Pay by credit card (Visa, Mastercard), through your bank or set up pre-authorized payments
    Check account balances
    Update your profile
    Learn about special offers and rewards

    Mobility customers can:
    Monitor monthly data, text and voice usage with Siri Shortcuts
    Download and view past bills
    Set data limits and turn overage protection on or off
    Customize add-ons, such as Easy Roam, international texting, data top-ups or Fast Pass

    Home Services customers can:
    Upgrade internet plan
    Monitor data usage
    Easily manage Pik TV and Optik TV channels, theme packs and premium selections
    Manage Wi-Fi

    Enjoying My TELUS? Please take a few minutes to rate us and tell us what you think.

    Please note:
    My TELUS requires iOS 13.0 or later.
    Select small business accounts are supported, but Corporate and Enterprise accounts are not yet eligible.
    My TELUS is optimized for iPhone.
