    Description

    Rogers is Canada’s largest and most reliable 5G network and fastest, most reliable internet provider, delivering seamless connectivity and lightning-fast speeds for all your needs. Get the MyRogers app so you can manage your account and services. It’s never been easier. Pay bills, track mobile data usage, set data alerts and log in securely to access your account safely, anytime and anywhere.

    Stay on top of your plan and usage
    Quickly check your plan details, upgrade options and account balance. Compare rates, review other plans and select add-ons to customize your services. You can also upgrade your device or check eligibility directly in the app.

    Track data usage and set alerts
    Keep an eye on your data usage with real-time tracking and set up notifications to avoid going over your data. Staying within your plan and budget has never been simpler.

    Manage SIM and eSIM
    • Activate, swap or manage your SIM or eSIM directly in the app.
    • Manage your family’s mobile lines all in one place to keep everyone connected.

    24/7 support anytime you need it
    • Get support whenever you need it with our robust selection of support articles.
    • Chat with our virtual assistant, always ready to help.

    Access to 5G network
    • Connect to Canada’s most reliable 5G network wherever you go.
    • Explore the latest 5G mobile plans from the app and make a switch.

    Travelling
    • If you’re travelling out of the country or province, you can check travel options and features on your plan
    • Turn roaming off or on
    • Choose when you to hold or suspend your service for a short while, if you’re going out of town.

    Exclusive Rogers Bank offers
    • Discover special offers from Rogers Bank directly through the app.
    • With the Rogers Red Mastercard, you can earn unlimited cash-back rewards on all your purchases, redeemable any where Mastercard is accepted.
    • Enjoy the benefits of no annual fee and get rewarded for everyday spending.

    Customize add-ons and services
    • Choose the add-ons you need, like device protection, extra data, long distance and voicemail.
    • Change your number or add roaming options to match your plans.

    Bundle and save
    • Combine your Rogers services to save more.
    • Check out streaming options and enjoy seamless connectivity across all your devices with Rogers Internet.

    Track orders and stay updated
    • Track your orders in real-time
    • Stay informed with our “What’s New” section to learn about the latest app features.

    Why choose the MyRogers app?
    • Manage account and services: Pay bills, check mobile data usage and set mobile data alerts
    • Secure login: Safe and easy access to your account
    • Plan and add-ons: Check plans, upgrade options, and customize add-ons
    • Device upgrades: Upgrade your device or check eligibility
    • SIM/eSIM: Activate or swap your SIM or eSIM seamlessly in the app
    • 24/7 support: Get help anytime with our virtual assistant
    • 5G network: Access Canada’s largest 5G network
    • Travel options: Manage roaming and travel settings on the go
    • Exclusive Rogers Bank offers
    • Family management: Manage all your family’s lines in one place
    • Bundle and save: Combine plans with Rogers Internet

    Download MyRogers app today to manage your Rogers account and services right at your fingertips and stay connected wherever you go.

    Much better but alerts need help

    I like the latest rendition. But I should be able to set an alert for any number. Not just an alert at the end of my regular data amount but also my promo data etc. What good is an alert that can’t alert me when I’m at 300 megs left for the month? Is it a corporate trap to encourage overages?

    Billing issues

    I have multiple accounts with Rogers. For the past two months I’ve been unable to pay my internet/cable bill. I keep getting an error message which is highly annoying. I have to call in to pay it over the phone. I can still pay my cell bill. Which is why I gave it 2 stars. Fix and upgrade your app features Rogers! It’s an inconvenience for a one- stop shop app!

    kuncca , 06/18/2018

    Now useless

    Prior to April 2016 this app worked for prepaid as well as monthly contract customers. After this upgrade the app would just error on login. For two years the bug was never acknowledged or fixed. Today, with the latest update the app now returns a message that it “does not support pay as you go”. I don’t know if we should be happy that the bug is finally acknowledged or disappointed that it apparently is never going to be fixed. Either way a poor approach to change management and customer support.
