What happens at your home is private — and it will stay that way. Our camera footage (live and recorded) can only be viewed by authenticated devices. It is already encrypted end-to-end by default, and when they are uploaded to the cloud, they're stored in our Canadian servers. End-to-end encryption is designed to encrypt these videos so that they can only be viewed on enrolled devices.
