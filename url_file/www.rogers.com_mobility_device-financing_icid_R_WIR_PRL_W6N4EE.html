How it works

Financing with a Rogers credit card

Lower your monthly device payments by up to 50%.1

  1. Buy your new phone in-store with your Rogers credit card and Rogers mobile services.
  2. Set up an Equal Payment Plan2 with 0% interest for 36 or 48 months.2

Not a Rogers credit card holder? Apply in-store and get approved on the spot.3

Financing with Rogers

  1. Choose any smartphone, tablet or smartwatch for $0 down on approved credit, 0% interest with financing over 24 months on a 5G Mobile plan or a Rogers Infinite plan.4
  2. Pay off your devices in equal monthly payments (plus taxes) over your 24-month term. You can also make lump-sum payments any time!

Financing with Save & Return

Get up to $570 Save & Return credit when you agree to return the device within your 24-month term.5

  1. Choose your phone and select financing with Save & Return as your financing option at checkout.
  2. Return the device to us within 24 months. Want to keep the device? Just pay the Save & Return amount and its yours!
